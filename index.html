YorkU

Unofficial app for York University

   

Screenshots

Created by York University Student for York University Students.

This app is meant to help you with navigating York University. Whether it be looking for course details or information regarding sections, or direction to your classes. York Uni App allows your to quickly look for washrooms, food places near you. With inclusion of Yu Study Buddies, the app allows you to find online groups and so much more!

With a lot of features:

  • Get Course Information
  • Get Directions to Classes
  • Find Nearby Food Places
  • Add Courses To Favorites
  • Find Nearby Washrooms
  • Widgets.. and more
